State Merit Scholarship Notice

Applications are invited from students studying Class 11 and Class 12. The student must have scored at least 75% marks in the previous academic year. Annual family income must be below Rs. 2,50,000.

Last date to apply: 30 June 2026 by 5:00 PM.

Required documents: Aadhaar card, income certificate, previous year marksheet, passport size photo, bank passbook copy.

Students who miss the deadline will not be considered for this scholarship cycle.
