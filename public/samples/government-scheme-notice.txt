Government Housing Support Scheme Notice

Eligible families can apply for housing support under the district welfare scheme. Applicant must be a resident of the district and annual household income must be below Rs. 3,00,000.

Applications must be submitted at the Mandal office by 15 July 2026.

Required documents include Aadhaar card, ration card, income certificate, residence certificate, and passport size photos. False information may lead to rejection and recovery of benefits.
